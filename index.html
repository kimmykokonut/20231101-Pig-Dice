Let's play some piggly-wiggly!

Rules

This is a dice game where you win if you are the first player to score 100 or more points

The first player rolls a die and scores as many points as the total shown on the die. You may continue rolling and accumulating points or end your turn by chosing the hold button. If you roll a 1, your score is 0 and your turn is over. Play passes to the next player and play continues until a winner is determined.

Player 1 goes first!
